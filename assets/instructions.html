Before You Go...

There are some things to know before you forge ahead on your epic quest!

  • Firstly, you will not need to enter ANY buidlings on this hunt, everything you will need to know is on the outside.
  • Secondly, the format of this hunt will be CLUE -> KEY -> NEXT CLUE and so on. Allow me to explain.
    1. When you receive a clue you will solve it and then head to that location.
    2. Once there you may hit the "I'm There!" button.
    3. This will unlock the "KEY" that will prove you have acutally arrived
    4. The key is a three letter combination that will come from some writing unique to that building. This three letter may include spaces!
    5. Once you put in that key correctly, you will unlock the next clue.
  • Do NOT refresh the page, this will cause you to skip a clue!
  • Use of the internet is allowed, but highly discouraged. Try to problem solve your way around the tough questions!
