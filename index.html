Davidson Scavenger Hunt

Already by being here you are a part of a very exclusive group of people. Davidson campus is a place of wonder and magic both in its history and beauty. But more than that, it is a place of intrigue. Walking the brick walkways you can feel a sense of purpose surrounding you. To feel that even more, you are now going to explore the campus in a way you never knew you could, via a grand Scavenger Hunt around the campus.
